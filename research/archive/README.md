# Archived Deep Research Reports

This directory stores completed deep-research reports after their selected findings have been integrated into chapter pages.

Archive rules:

- Move a report here only after the relevant chapter-category targets are marked `integrated` in `research/status.yml`.
- Keep archived reports committed as high-value source artifacts.
- Do not delete archived reports when chapter opportunities are edited, reset, or removed.
- After moving a report from `research/completed/` to `research/archive/`, update every affected `completed_report` path in `research/status.yml`.
- If integrated results are later disputed, preserve the archived report and mark affected targets `needs-rerun` or `reset` with explanatory notes.
