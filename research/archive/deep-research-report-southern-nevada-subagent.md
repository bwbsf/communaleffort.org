# Deep Research Report: BWB Southern Nevada Collaboration Opportunities

Last verified: 2026-06-24

## Summary

Southern Nevada has strong collaboration potential around practical volunteer mobilization, public-space stewardship, food and service navigation, creative placemaking, and community education. The strongest leads are organizations with active public programs and clear local infrastructure: First Friday Foundation for arts-district activation, Three Square for hunger-relief logistics, Get Outdoors Nevada for stewardship events, United Way of Southern Nevada and Engelstad Foundation for funding/community-resiliency infrastructure, EJATC/IBEW for skilled-trades learning, Habitat ReStore and SYN Shop for reuse/build capacity, City of Las Vegas Neighborhood Services and Clark County Parks & Recreation for neighborhood cleanups and public facilities, Nevada Homeless Alliance for cross-provider coordination, ACDC for multilingual community-based outreach, and Las Vegas-Clark County Library District for public programming venues and maker/STEAM infrastructure.

The main validation limit is that this report used public web evidence only. Before outreach or import, a maintainer should verify current contact pathways, partnership openness, geographic fit for the Southern Nevada chapter, and whether each organization is comfortable being framed as a BWB collaboration lead.
