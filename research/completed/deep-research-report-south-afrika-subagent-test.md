# Collaboration Opportunities for BWB South Afrika — Subagent Test

## Summary

BWB South Afrika appears to be best treated as a South Africa-wide chapter with a practical Western Cape / Cape Town / Tankwa Karoo center of gravity until a human confirms a more specific operating base. The strongest collaboration patterns are: burner-adjacent volunteer mobilization through AfrikaBurn, public art and placemaking in Cape Town, food and shelter logistics through established direct-service providers, environmental stewardship and coastal cleanup partners, formal disaster-preparedness channels, maker/fabrication capacity, and resident-led civic organizations that can prevent top-down project design.

This test run found credible leads for all requested categories. Several leads are countrywide or Western Cape/Cape Town centered rather than strictly tied to a single South Afrika chapter city, so the main validation caveat is geographic fit. A maintainer should confirm whether BWB South Afrika should prioritize Cape Town, Tankwa Karoo / Northern Cape, Johannesburg, Durban, or national collaborations before importing all entries.
