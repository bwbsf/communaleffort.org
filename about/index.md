Communal Effort maps Burners Without Borders chapters and potential local collaborators.

The project is intentionally practical. It aims to help chapters and researchers understand:

- where chapters are active or historically relevant,
- what kinds of work those chapters appear to do,
- which local organizations may be useful collaborators,
- and which sources support each claim.

The deeper background reference is available at [Background]({{ '/background/' | relative_url }}).
