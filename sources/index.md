---
title: Sources
description: Source standards for chapter and collaborator research.
permalink: /sources/
---

Each chapter and collaborator record should include source links that support the claim being made.

Preferred sources:

- official Burners Without Borders pages,
- official Burning Man Project pages and annual reports,
- official BWB chapter directory entries,
- official BWB project pages,
- local chapter websites or public channels,
- public agency pages,
- nonprofit and community organization websites,
- community foundation and fiscal sponsor websites,
- local news only when primary sources are unavailable.

Use normal Markdown links and keep source notes close to the data they verify.
