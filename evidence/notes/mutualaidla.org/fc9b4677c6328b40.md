> # Home

> Mutual Aid LA Network (MALAN) is a connector and information hub for mutual aid efforts, people and resources across Los Angeles. We’re building toward abolition and believe in an abundant world that can be freed of oppression through community solidarity.
>
> We have our own projects (https://mutualaidla.org/projects/) you’re welcome to join in on like Produce in the Park (https://mutualaidla.org/projects/produce-in-the-park/) , Hygiene Hello! (https://mutualaidla.org/projects/hygiene-hello/) , The Dispatch (https://mutualaidla.org/projects/dispatch/) , our Poli-Ed Reading Group (https://mutualaidla.org/projects/reading-group/) , and more. We are grateful for every donation (https://hcb.hackclub.com/donations/start/malan) as they keep these projects running, cover our operating costs, or are redistributed to other mutual aid efforts across LA. We welcome you and thank you for your interest!
>
> Plug in or find resources with our Group Listings Directory (https://mutualaidla.org/listings/) (currently offline), which pulls up-to-date info and opportunities from mutual aid groups listed in The Dispatch (https://dispatch.mutualaidla.org/) . Our main goal is to make it easy for you to find a mutual aid practicing group that fits, based on location, schedule, resource offered, remote or in-person needs, and area of interest or skill.
>
> If you have a resource to add/edit/remove OR are an org needing item donations & volunteers…. .please submit this form (http://tiny.cc/malan-catalog-form) .
