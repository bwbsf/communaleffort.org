> ## Calgary's Leading Voice in Zero Waste and Plastic Free Living
>
> Plastic-Free YYC was a nonprofit organisation leading Calgarians to reduce single-use plastic waste and create a circular economy (2018 - 2024).
> Plasticfreeyy.com remains a free educational resource comprised of plastic-free and zero-waste topics to support your journey in reducing waste.
>
> ## What we’re doing now
>
> Plastic-Free YYC was founded by Briana Loughlin in 2017 as a blog about plastic-free living. Between 2018 and 2024, this simple blog grew into a thriving voice in Calgary, advocating for a reduction in single-use plastic waste and inspiring change across our city.
>
> More than 100 dedicated volunteers worked hard to bring Plastic-Free YYC missions to life. These dedicated volunteers inspired individuals, collaborated with businesses, and worked with government leaders to create a more sustainable future. Engaging in collaborative initiatives, the team at Plastic-Free YYC joined forces with other grassroots organisations such as Ban the Bag YYC and The Last Straw (Toronto, Edmonton, Calgary), making significant impacts. We are incredibly proud of what we have achieved as a community.
