> Cambridge Hackspace
>
> ### what
>
> We are a 501(c)3 makerspace in the center of Somerville, MA. We have a 1200sqft workshop catering to enthusiastic makers, and hold weekly meetings where we get together and make things. We have a Laser cutter, CNCs, 3D printers, and a range of other tools for our members to use.
>
> ### why
>
> We've started the Cambridge Hackspace to provide a physical space where people can gather and work on their projects, have access to larger or expensive tools (like the 3D printers, and laser cutter), and provide a place where the community can share their knowledge.
>
> ### who
>
> Anyone and everyone is welcome. Whether your thing is software, electronics, woodwork, or knitting, we provide a space where you can meet fellow makers, to learn, and be inspired. We hold workshops every month so you can learn new skills.
