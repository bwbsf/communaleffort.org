> Bertha House works off a paid use and reciprocity model for space usage. We ask the collectives with financial means to pay to use our facilities. This enables us to host non-paying individuals or groups, ensuring access to essential resources and facilitating the curation of programming for the space. Those who don’t have financial means are invited to give back to the space through reciprocity by sharing their skills (run a workshop, tutor, teach a class etc) for the benefit of the rest of the Bertha House community. It is in this way that all users of the space are invited to join us in embodying radical hospitality.
